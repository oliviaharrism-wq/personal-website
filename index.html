About me

PM at the intersection of data, strategy, and people

I'm a Senior Technical Product Manager at Nike, where I work on supply chain technology in the materials planning domain — helping Nike understand, plan, and strategically source the materials that go into every shoe and piece of apparel.

What I do best is take a broad, ambiguous question — "how do we improve quality?" or "how do we improve the patient experience?" — and turn it into a concrete, executable plan. That means synthesizing external market data with internal insights, stakeholder goals, business capabilities, and company strategy to build a cohesive vision for how technology can solve problems at scale.

I've worked across a range of environments — from Series B health tech startups through IPO to multinational public companies like Nike — and that range has shaped how I approach product. It's taught me how to connect user problems to real business outcomes, and to balance speed with rigor depending on the context.

I've developed a strong appreciation for the unglamorous work behind great products: user research, financial modeling, ruthless prioritization, cross-functional alignment, prototyping, and clear writing. I value building and testing early ideas through prototypes, betas, and A/B testing to drive rapid iteration and validate direction based on real user behavior.

Most recently at Nike, I've run end-to-end user research with 30+ senior leaders across supply chain operations, built financial models that unlocked executive investment, and developed AI prototypes that reimagine how factories inspect quality throughout production. Before Nike, I built my product foundation at Phreesia, Reify Health, and Imagen Technologies, working across patient experience, data platforms, and 0→1 product development.

My focus is simple: build products that work in the real world, drive measurable impact, and achieve meaningful adoption.

Outside of work, I'm a new runner — having completed my first half marathon at the Napa Valley Half Marathon — and I love all forms of media, from books and podcasts to movies and music. I also enjoy exploring Portland's renowned food scene.
